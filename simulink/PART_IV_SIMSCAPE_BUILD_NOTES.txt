PART IV SIMSCAPE BUILD NOTES
============================
Build the actual RLC circuit in Simscape Electrical using:
- Pulse Voltage Source (or Controlled Voltage Source)
- Inductor: 9 H
- Capacitor: 800 uF to ground at the middle node
- Inductor: 3 H
- Resistor: 100 ohms to ground
- Electrical Reference
- Solver Configuration
- Voltage Sensor + PS-Simulink Converter
- To Workspace blocks

Suggested exported variables:
- t_sim
- x_sim
- y_sim

Then run plot_simscape_export.m to regenerate the Part IV and Part V figures from the real Simscape output.
